PhD Students

I will be considering PhD student applications for Fall 2027. I am looking for people who are genuinely interested in the research described on this site. Experience conducting related research is appreciated, but not required.

I enjoy working with scientists who are intellectually curious, willing to engage deeply with both theory and methods, and comfortable with uncertainty and failure as part of the research process. I welcome applicants from all backgrounds, including those who have taken non-traditional paths. I believe that diverse perspectives make our science better.

If this sounds like you, please reach out! Send an email introducing yourself, describing your research interests, and explaining why you think our lab would be a good fit. You are encouraged to email before formally applying. I'd love to chat about whether the lab might be a good match for you.

Email Dr. Wohltjen

Undergraduate Research Assistants

We welcome undergraduate students from any school or major who are curious about social psychology, human behavior, and scientific research. No prior research experience is necessary to apply.

If you are interested in working with us, fill out the application form below, send your CV or resume to socialcoordinationlab@gmail.com, and we will contact you to schedule an interview.
